Caprese Skewer's Recipe

Caprese Skewer Description

This recipe is a much more lighter snack for the holiday season.

Caprese Skewer Ingredients:

  • Grape Tomatoes
  • Cubed Mozzarella Cheese
  • Virgin olive oil
  • Basil Leaves
  • Salt
  • Black pepper

Steps to Make:

  1. Mix cheese, olive oil, tomatoes, basil, salt, pepper in bowl.
  2. Use a wooden skewer to poke the tomatoes and cheese
  3. Enjoy your meal!
