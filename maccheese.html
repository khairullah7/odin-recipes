Mac and Cheese Recipe

Mac and Cheese Description:

Mac and Cheese is another great thanksgiving meal that goes well with other dishes. This warm and toasty meal will taste amazing during the holdiay season!

Ingredients

  • Elbow macaroni
  • Cheese sliced
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Ground black pepper

Steps to Make:

  1. Heat oven to 300 Degrees.
  2. Layer macaroni and cheese on top of each other.
  3. Mix eggs and milk and put over macaroni.
  4. Bake in oven until right consistency and season with pepper afterwards.
